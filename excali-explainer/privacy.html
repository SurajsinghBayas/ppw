Privacy Policy

At Java Mastery, we value your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your personal data when you interact with our platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you register on Java Mastery, we collect standard credentials including your Name, Email Address, and a Hashed Password. Passwords are encrypted immediately using bcrypt on the server, meaning we do not store, and cannot view, your actual plain-text password.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Create and maintain your user account.
  • Enable secure login and session management (JWT tokens).
  • Maintain database association of your profile details.

We do NOT track your browsing habits, sell your data to third parties, or send promotional spam emails.

3. Data Storage & Security

Our serverless database backend is hosted securely on Neon DB. While we use standard best practices to secure your data (e.g. hashing passwords, HTTPS traffic redirection), no transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use unique passwords.

4. Cookies and Session Storage

We use your browser's local storage or session storage to keep you logged in between sessions (so you do not have to log in every time you open the app). We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

5. Contact Us

If you wish to have your account deleted or have questions regarding your data, you can reach out via our GitHub repository issues panel.
